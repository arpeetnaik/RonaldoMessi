Lionel Messi

Born in the city of Rosario, Messi was born with extraordinary talents that would make him shine and earn a spot at Barcelona at a young age.

As Argentina's economy was collapsing, Messi was noticed while playing for his then team, Newell's Old Boys. Barcelona signed him and offered to pay his medical bills if he was willing to move to Spain at 13 years old.
