Cristiano Ronaldo

Born in the city of Funchal, this futbol superstar grew up in poverty with almost nothing to call his own.

Ronaldo quickly grew to stardom and at only 12 years old, was pushed to leave his family to play at Sporting Lisbon academy to chase his football career.
