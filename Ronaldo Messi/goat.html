The GOATS of Futbol

👑 Cristiano Ronaldo and Lionel Messi 👑

Through their whole career, their willingness to stay humble and work hard is the reason they are who they are.
From Ronaldo losing his father early in his career, to Messi with his lack of height, neither one of them let anything stop them from being the best.
Award and Accolades are cool, but their knowledge of the game and the ability to carry their teams is what really sets them apart.
Many still debate on who the best of both is, but in my eyes they should both be respected for what they've done for the game.
As their careers come to a close soon, there will be something missing from the game that nobody will be able to replicate.
And for that reason, and that reason only.

They are the GOAT's
